# Code of Conduct

## Our standard

Participate with respect, care, and professionalism. Harassment, discrimination, intimidation, and sharing customer data without authorisation are not tolerated.

## Reporting

Report conduct concerns privately to a repository maintainer. Do not publish personal information or customer material in an issue or discussion. Maintainers will acknowledge reports, assess them fairly, and may remove content, restrict participation, or take other proportionate action.

This standard applies to all project spaces and events.
